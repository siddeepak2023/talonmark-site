ClientFalcon vs AgencyAnalytics vs Swydo

AgencyAnalytics and Swydo are established connector platforms — they pull live data and arrange it into dashboards and scheduled reports. ClientFalcon starts one step later: it computes the numbers and writes the client narrative, so the deliverable is a report someone already wrote, not a layout waiting for commentary.

When AgencyAnalytics or Swydo is the better pick

  • Clients expect live dashboards they can log into anytime.
  • You report across many niche channels and need a deep connector catalog.
  • Your team already has the narrative workflow down and just needs layout + scheduling.

When ClientFalcon is the better pick

  • The expensive part of your reporting is the writing — the account-lead hours, not the charts.
  • You want every sentence in the narrative checked against the computed numbers it cites.
  • You want to run the whole roster in one batch and edit for voice, not assemble from scratch.
  • You'd rather not request OAuth access to client accounts to produce a monthly PDF.
