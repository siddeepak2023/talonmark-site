Frequently asked

How is talonmark different from AgencyAnalytics or Whatagraph?›

Those are static dashboards — they show numbers, but you still write the analysis. talonmark is AI-native: it computes the metrics and writes the narrative ("ROAS down 18% on Meta; cost-per-result up 31% since the Mar 4 creative change") and generates the full written report. You review, not rebuild.

Which platforms does talonmark connect to?›

Meta Ads, Google Ads, and GA4 — the stack for most small-to-mid marketing agencies. Drop a CSV export today; more platforms (TikTok, LinkedIn, Klaviyo) on the roadmap.

Can I white-label the reports?›

Yes — reports carry your agency logo and brand color. The "Powered by talonmark" badge is subtle on Solo; Agency and Studio plans give full brand control.

Is my client data safe?›

The report builder parses your file in the browser — nothing is uploaded to a server. Encrypted, isolated per account, never used for model training.
