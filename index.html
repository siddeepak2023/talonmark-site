FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Do I have to connect my clients' ad accounts?
No. ClientFalcon runs on the CSV exports you already pull from Meta, Google Ads, and GA4 — no OAuth, no pixel, no access to client accounts. Direct connectors are in review; until they ship, it's exports, and we say so.
Will my client know I used ClientFalcon?
No. The PDF carries your logo and brand colors, and share links are unbranded. White-label means white-label.
Can Autopilot change my campaigns without asking?
No. Autopilot queues pause/scale recommendations with the math behind each one, and every action waits for your explicit approval. Nothing touches an ad account on its own.
Are the numbers in the narrative real?
Yes — the prose is AI-drafted, but every figure is computed from your exports with real code. The narrative can't cite a number the computation didn't produce.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — one click from your billing page. Your plan stays active until the end of the period you paid for, and generated reports remain downloadable.
Which ad platforms are supported?
Meta Ads, Google Ads, and GA4 via standard CSV exports — dropped in together for one blended report.
